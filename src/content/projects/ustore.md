## Project Overview

Ustore is an e-commerce solution within the UPayments ecosystem that empowers merchants to launch their own professional online stores without coding knowledge.

As a Frontend Developer, I was responsible for building the storefront engine and the customization capabilities that allow merchants to tailor their shops to their brand.

### Key Features & Contributions

- **Customizable Layouts:** Developed a dynamic theming system offering **7 distinct layouts**, giving merchants the flexibility to change the look and feel of their store instantly.
- **E-commerce Engine:** Built the core shopping experience, including product listings, shopping cart, and checkout flows integrated with UPayments gateway.
- **Responsive Design:** Ensured all store themes are fully responsive, providing an optimal shopping experience on mobile and desktop.
- **Merchant Configuration:** Created intuitive settings interfaces for merchants to manage their store's appearance and content.

### Tech Stack

- **Framework:** Next.js
- **Language:** TypeScript
- **State Management:** Redux
- **Styling:** Tailwind CSS
